Welcome to Trenton Madden's portfolio site, and future location of The E Project.

Trenton is a Navy veteran and a current Mechanical Engineering student at the University of North Carolina, Charlotte. Trenton is not your average engineering student; he is a dreamer and laser focused on his goal of changing the educational status quo for young people. He feels many people in his generation lack job skills and motivation needed to be successful. He feels many people settle in their lives due to fear of becoming something greater.

Trenton's the kind of person who is a natural leader, and his peer group would consider him to be the most driven individual they know. Trenton strives to overcome barriers. When he finishes school, he will be looking for an employer who expects these qualities in their team.
